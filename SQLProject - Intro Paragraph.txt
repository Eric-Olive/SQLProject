	Do you think going outside sucks and wish you could make other people do it for you. Well now you can. Forty-Niner Diner Delivery is a service designed by people with agoraphobia
for people with agoraphobia. All orders will be place either through the Forty-Niner Diner Mobile Application or alternatively through the My UNCC website under dining options. Orders will
be made exclusively from on-campus dining services. This includes restaurants within Prospector, Cone, the Student Union, etc, and the Dining Halls (Crown and Sovi). Deliveries will be transported through
various means including walking, bus, bike, or, if licensed and applicable, car. There will be specified drop off locations on-campus that each order must be picked up from.
